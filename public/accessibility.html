Accessibility Statement
Commitment to Accessible Application Process and Customer Service

W&A Professional Corporation is committed to accommodating applicants with disabilities, up to the point of undue hardship, during the recruitment, assessment and selection process. Please notify W&A Professional Corporation if you require accommodation in respect of the materials or procedures used at any time during this process. If you require accommodation W&A Professional Corporation will work with you to determine how to meet your needs.

W&A Professional Corporation is committed to providing customer service in a manner accessible to all of our clients and prospective clients, including those with a disability. To achieve this goal we have implemented policies, procedures and practices to help make the services we offer more accessible.
